FAQ

QUESTIONS WE GET EVERY WEEK.

How much is a lift kit for a RAM 1500?+
A BDS 3" kit fitted (including alignment, fitment warranty and any required upper control arms) starts at $4,200. A 5" Fox Performance Elite kit fitted starts at $6,800. We give you the full price before we order parts.
Will lifting my truck void the warranty?+
Not when fitted by a specialist workshop using authorised parts. We document the build so the dealer can see exactly what was done and keep your factory warranty intact wherever possible.
Is it legal to lift a truck in Queensland?+
Yes — up to 50mm without engineering certification, and over that with QLD heavy vehicle modifications certification (which we organise as part of the build).
Can you source parts for American trucks in Australia?+
Yes. We've built a parts network specifically for RAM, Silverado, F-150 and GMC. Genuine and quality OEM, correctly specced for your truck rather than substituting incompatible alternatives.
Do you fit parts I've bought elsewhere?+
Yes — but the warranty on the install is reduced to labour only. We always recommend going through our supplier network for the full package workmanship warranty.
